Our Services

Professional Window Cleaning
Inside & Out

Our professional window cleaning service restores clarity, removes dirt and buildup, and leaves your windows streak-free inside and out.

Exterior Glass Cleaning

We thoroughly clean all exterior glass surfaces, removing dirt, pollen, hard water deposits, and oxidation for crystal-clear results you can see from the street.

Interior Glass Cleaning

Optional interior window cleaning available for full-service results. We use streak-free solutions and professional-grade squeegees for a flawless finish inside your home.

Window Frame Cleaning

We wipe down and clean all window frames and casings to complement your sparkling glass — because details matter in professional window cleaning.

Window Sill Cleaning

Dusty, grimy sills are cleaned and wiped down as part of our standard service, leaving your entire window area looking brand new.

Track Cleaning

Available upon request. We deep-clean window tracks that collect dirt, debris, and insects — ensuring smooth operation and a thorough clean throughout.

Screen Cleaning

Available upon request. Window screens are removed, cleaned, and reinstalled with care — removing pollen and buildup that blocks your view and light.

How much does window cleaning cost?
Pricing varies based on the number of windows, accessibility, and services requested. We provide free, no-obligation quotes — contact us at 417-371-6798 or fill out our online form to get your personalized estimate.
Do you clean interior and exterior windows?
Yes! We offer exterior-only cleaning or full interior + exterior service. Just let us know what you need when you request your quote and we'll customize the service for your home or business.
How often should I get my windows cleaned?
For most homes, we recommend cleaning 2–4 times per year. Businesses and storefronts often benefit from monthly service. We're happy to set up a recurring schedule that works for your budget and property.
What areas do you serve?
We serve Nixa, Springfield, Ozark, Republic, Battlefield, Willard, Rogersville, and all surrounding communities in the 417 area. Not sure if we come to you? Give us a call — chances are we do!
Do you clean screens and tracks?
Yes, screen cleaning and track cleaning are both available upon request. Just mention it when booking and we'll include it in your quote.
Are you insured?
Yes. ClearPane Exteriors operates as a fully insured business so you have complete peace of mind while we work on your property. We take every precaution to protect your home and our team.
Do I need to be home during the cleaning?
For exterior-only service, you typically don't need to be home. For interior cleaning, we do ask that someone is present. We'll coordinate all details with you in advance so everything goes smoothly.
